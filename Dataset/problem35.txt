### **Joint Backhaul and Access Link Optimization**

### **Problem Description:**

In heterogeneous networks, backhaul links must be optimized along with access links to improve network efficiency.

**Objective:** Maximize the total network throughput while maintaining backhaul constraints.

**Constraints:**

1. The total backhaul capacity must not be exceeded.
2. Each user must have a minimum required data rate.

**Variable Parameters:**

- Number of small cells: 5
- Backhaul capacity per small cell: 500 Mbps
- Minimum data rate per user: 5 Mbps
- Total number of users: 30
