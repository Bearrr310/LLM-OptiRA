### Power Control for Interference Mitigation Problem
**Problem Description**
This optimization problem focuses on minimizing the total interference experienced by users in a wireless network by optimally allocating power among transmitters. The objective is to enhance overall network performance while adhering to specific SINR requirements.

### Variable Parameters
1. **Number of Users**: 5
2. **Maximum Transmit Power per User**: 25 Watts
3. **Total Available Power**: 150 Watts
4. **Minimum SINR Requirement**: 12 dB
5. **Channel Gain Formula**: Expressed as:
\[
G_{ij} = \frac{1}{(d_{ij})^{\gamma}} \quad (\gamma = 2.2, d_{ij} \text{ is the distance between transmitter } i \text{ and receiver } j)
\]
6. **Noise Variance**: \(N_0 = 1 \times 10^{-9}\) Watts
