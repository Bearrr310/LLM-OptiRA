### **Energy-Efficient RIS-Assisted Communication**

### **Problem Description:**

Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces (RIS) can reflect signals to improve coverage. The challenge is to optimize the phase shifts of RIS elements.

**Objective:** Minimize the total power consumption while achieving a target SINR.

**Constraints:**

1. The phase shift of each RIS element must be within a given range.
2. The total transmitted power must not exceed a limit.

**Variable Parameters:**

- Number of RIS elements: 64
- Number of users: 6
- Maximum transmit power: 25 dBm
- Minimum SINR per user: 12 dB
